
# Phone SIM contracts

For the last three years a Bangladeshi man living in Saudi Arabia has been using my email as his main email for signing up to mobile plans. 
So far there's numbers for Virgin, Mobily, Orange etc. Quite significant plans too - often hundreds of Saudi Riyals.

On the confirmation of his SIM card plan it included his Iqama number, name, signature and now mobile number; the Iqama 
number in particular is very important, as this is Saudi Arabia's digital identification for expatriates. This is the kind of information 
I wouldn't be quick to share with a stranger unless I was trying to speedrun identity theft.
